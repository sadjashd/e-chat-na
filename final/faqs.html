FAQ
  • What is E-Chat-Na?

    E-Chat-Na is a chat system to help the users translate mathematical equations into wordy equations. It will help the users understand math better with the help of live chat with volunteers. We used Messenger's Chat System that would be flexible for everyone. You can continue as guests, or save your work by logging in with your Facebook account. Additionally, It will not also require you to buy load, as it works as long your data is on.

  • How does it work?

    By tapping onto the messenger chat icon below, it will direct you to talk to one of our admins, or volunteers.

  • How did you thought of E-Chat-Na?

    E-Chat-Na was supposed to be a scanning web tool, however, with the lack of manpower and programming knowledge, we have thought of alternatives that wouldn't require too much work.

  • What are some of the Math related topics can you answer?

    We can help with Arithmetic equations, such as addition, subtraction, multiplication, division, fraction, etc. On the other hand, we can translate some algebra-related questions, statistics and probability, and some equations under pre-calculus.

  • Why is your help limited?

    We wanted to reach out to help everybody, But since, not every one of us is good at math and the knowledge of some of us is limited, we have thought of asking some volunteers to help us in answering your queries.
